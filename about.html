About Antony

Antony Matheus dos Santos, commonly known as Antony, is a Brazilian footballer known for his electric pace, dribbling ability, and relentless determination on the field. He currently plays as a winger for Manchester United and the Brazilian national team. Born in São Paulo, Antony’s rise to fame has been meteoric, with his impressive performances drawing attention worldwide.
