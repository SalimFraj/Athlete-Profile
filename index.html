Antony Matheus dos Santos

Antony is a Brazilian professional footballer who plays as a winger for Manchester United and Brazil's national team. Known for his dribbling skills, Antony has become a rising star in European football.

"Antony's impact has been phenomenal since his arrival. His technique and flair are exceptional." - Football Analyst

Born in São Paulo, Brazil, Antony started his professional career at São Paulo FC before moving to Ajax and later to Manchester United in 2022. He has since been pivotal in the Red Devils' attacking strategy.
